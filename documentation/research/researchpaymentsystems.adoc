= Research Payment Systems for Cafeteria Ordering Application
:toc:
:icons: font

== Objective

The purpose of this document is to examine multiple payment system alternatives by presenting an overview of each provider, conducting a structured comparative evaluation of their technical and operational characteristics, assessing their suitability for integration into the Cafeteria Ordering application, and providing a final recommendation based on the analysis.


== Payment Providers Selected
Based on market relevance and wide compatibility with applications, the following payment providers were selected:

* Provider 1: Stripe
* Provider 2: PayPal
* Provider 3: Square
* Provider 4: Braintree
* Provider 5: Adyen

== Provider Overview

=== Stripe
* Description:
Stripe is a technology-focused payment processing platform that provides APIs and SDKs for accepting online and mobile payments.

* Company background:
Founded in 2010 in the United States, Stripe has become one of the leading global payment processors, serving startups and large enterprises.

* Primary use case:
E-commerce websites, SaaS platforms, subscription services, and mobile applications.

=== PayPal
* Description:
PayPal is an online payment system that allows users and businesses to send and receive payments electronically.

* Company background:
Founded in 1998, PayPal is one of the most established digital payment companies worldwide.

* Primary use case:
E-commerce platforms, peer-to-peer payments, and online marketplaces.


=== Square
* Description:
Square is a financial services platform that provides payment processing and point-of-sale (POS) solutions.

* Company background:
Founded in 2009, Square initially focused on small business POS systems and later expanded into online payments.

* Primary use case:
In-person POS systems, restaurants, small businesses, and online payment processing.


=== Braintree
* Description:
Braintree is a full-stack payment platform that provides online and mobile payment processing and supports multiple payment methods.

* Company background:
Founded in 2007 and acquired by PayPal in 2013, Braintree focuses on providing developer-friendly solutions for online payments and subscriptions.

* Primary use case:
Mobile apps, e-commerce platforms, subscription services.


=== Adyen
* Description:
Adyen is a global payment company providing a single platform to accept payments online, in-app, and in-store.

* Company background:
Founded in 2006 in the Netherlands, Adyen serves large enterprises and is known for its seamless global payment solutions.

* Primary use case:
Enterprise e-commerce, omnichannel retail, and in-person payments.

== 6. Pros and Cons

=== Provider 1: Stripe

* Pros:
- Excellent React Native support
- Modern APIs
- High-quality documentation
- Advanced fraud detection tools

* Cons:
- Slight learning curve for beginners

=== Provider 2: PayPal

* Pros:
- Strong global brand recognition
- Large customer trust
- Convenient for users with PayPal accounts

* Cons:
- Less flexible mobile SDK
- Possible account holds in certain cases

=== Provider 3: Square

* Pros:
- Ideal for cafeteria POS systems
- Strong hardware integration
- Competitive in-person transaction rates

* Cons:
- More focused on physical POS systems
- Online tools less flexible compared to others

=== Provider 4: Braintree

* Pros:
- Supports multiple payment methods including PayPal and Venmo
- Developer-friendly APIs
- Recurring billing and subscription support

* Cons:
- React Native integration requires additional work
- Slightly more complex to set up than others

=== Provider 5: Adyen

* Pros:
- Enterprise-grade platform with global payment support
- Wide range of local payment methods
- Strong fraud detection and risk management

* Cons:
- Pricing can be complex for small businesses
- More suited for large-scale enterprise integration

== 7. Final Recommendation

* Recommended provider: Stripe

* Justification: Stripe is overall the best alternative for the project due to its high compatibility with React Native, easier Supabase integration, modern APIs and  SDKs, high-quality security features, and developer-friendly experience.
