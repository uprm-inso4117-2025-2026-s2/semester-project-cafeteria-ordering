== Overview

This document compiles a list of food items commonly found in a cafeteria along with their respective ingredients.

The purpose of this list is to support the *menu screen* of the ordering system by providing representative food items and their configurable ingredient options.

For the purposes of the cafeteria ordering system, only *optional or configurable ingredients* are listed. Base ingredients required for a product to exist (for example, coffee in a cup of coffee or bread in a sandwich) are intentionally omitted to avoid redundancy when creating food items in the database.

== Usage and Summary

Eight cafeteria food items were identified and documented, each including configurable or optional ingredients that may vary per order.

Required base ingredients for each product were intentionally omitted to prevent redundancy and simplify the database representation of menu items.

These items can serve as initial seed data for the cafeteria ordering system, supporting features such as:

- menu display
- ingredient customization
- ingredient tracking
- order configuration
